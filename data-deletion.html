Delete All Data & Account

Option A: In-App (Recommended)

Steps:
  1. Open the Salva app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap "Delete My Data"
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What happens: All your account data, household data, content alerts, device records, and cloud-stored information will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Option B: Email Request
